Details

The college has separate hostels for boys and girls on the campus where 250 boys and 500 girls can be comfortably accommodated. Residents live like a family here helping one another at the time of need. They are assisted by the hostel staff to make them feel at home.Hostels are provided with 24x7 water supply along with hot water.
There are three two types available:

  • AC

  • NON-AC

The fee charges may differ acoordingly on the basis of the type of room the student is taking. The AC rooms are provided on the ground floors of two hostels only.
The three hostel buildings:
  • H1
  • H2
  • NH
We have two separate study rooms with sufficient lighting and conditioning. Students can study anytime they want to since it is available to them the whole day.
